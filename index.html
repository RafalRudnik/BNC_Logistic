About

BNC Rafal Rudnik international forwarding company founded in 2017. The company's registered office is located at: Obroncow Wybrzeza 3A/34 str. 80-398 Gdansk Poland. The owner has over 18 years of experience in delivering goods in an international chain in Europe and beyond. We have experience in driving and technical care of trucks. We have been involved in deliveries over the years goods under time pressure, for commodity shares of such large companies as Lidl or Aldi. In cooperation, we value trust, loyalty and friendly relations. Payments without downtime. We invest our time in long-term business relationships. If you are interested in cooperation, please contact us via the form in the contact section.

Profile

We import and export goods from and to Poland and the Czech Republic. The main direction of these operations is Western Europe.

We participate in the supply chain of goods to ports where it is reloaded and prepared for the sea route.

Whether you are a large or small producer, it does not matter to us. Everyone counts.

We have extensive experience in the transport of refrigerated goods that require temperature control.
